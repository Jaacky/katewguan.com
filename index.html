What is the meaning of life?

How do we live meaningful lives?

How do the relationships we partake in, the moral we believe, and the cultural norms we adopt, help us construct and maintain a sense of meaning?

I'm a first year master's student in psychology at the University of British Columbia. I work with Dr. Steven Heine, and my research explores the intersections between culture, morality, meaning, and relationships.

On this website, I feature my academic work and profile. I also blog regularly on a number of topics spanning personal thoughts to critical explorations of the path to becoming a research psychologist.
